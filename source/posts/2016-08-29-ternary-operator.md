Ternary operator (`?:`) ile basit koşullu ifadeler yazabiliriz.

```js
condition ? value1 : value2
```
READ_MORE

Örnek:

```js
let age = 18;
let status = (age >= 18) ? "Adult" : "Minor" // ## Adult
```
